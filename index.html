Biography

Erkun Yang is currently a PhD candidate in School of Electronic Engineering, Xidian University, under the supervision of Prof. Cheng Deng and Prof. Jie Li, and he is a visiting student in UBTECH Sydney AI Centre, University of Sydney (USYD), under the supervision of Prof. Dacheng Tao from Mar 2017. He received his bachelor degree in Xidian University in July, 2013. His research interests are Deep Learning, Similarity Search, and Learn to Hash.
